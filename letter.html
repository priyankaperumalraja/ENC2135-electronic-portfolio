Letter to Senator Tammy Duckworth

Senator Tammy Duckworth U.S. Senate 524 Hart Senate Office Building Washington, DC 20510 Dear Senator Duckworth, My name is Priyanka Perumalraja, and I am currently a Computer Science university student passionate about making technology accessible to everyone in the United States, regardless of ability or access whether that be because of digital illiteracy or a lack of technology and technology education resources. I am writing to you to urge your support toward creating and funding initiatives that will foster the hindrance of the digital divide and bring about universal access to basic technology and technology education resources such as high-speed broadband internet, laptops, cellphones, data processing software, and digital literacy, regardless of financial ability, location, and previous knowledge. With your history of supporting tech-advancement initiatives such as the Illinois Tech Hubs and the bill you introduced to help grow the number of people in our armed forces have a background in technology, I am confident in your ability to further this goal and improve technology access and education in the United States. Thank you for your time. Kind regards, Priyanka Perumalraja Cawthon Hall, Honors Way
